Biodiversity is the *variety* of life on Earthâ€”all the plants, animals, and microorganisms, and the ecosystems they form. Think of it as a giant, colorful puzzle!

Genetic Diversity

The variety *within* a species (like all the different types of dogs or apples). It keeps populations healthy.

Species Diversity

The variety of different species in an area (like lions, butterflies, trees, and mushrooms). It makes ecosystems strong.

Ecosystem Diversity

The variety of places where life can be found (like rainforests, oceans, deserts, and wetlands). It supports all life.
